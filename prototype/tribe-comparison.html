Experiment 03

Space Isolation Context

Does framing matter? Same ocean audio with warm biophilic description vs clinical description, plus isolation text without audio. Tests whether Solace's warm, Earth-connected tone produces measurably different brain activation than a clinical approach.

Condition A: Biophilic

Ocean audio + warm Earth-connected text about home, sand, waves

Condition B: Clinical

Key Finding

Even with identical audio content, the accompanying text framing changes how the brain processes the experience. Biophilic framing — evoking Earth, home, nature — should show distinct activation patterns compared to cold clinical language. The isolation-only condition reveals the neural impact of feeling disconnected, which is exactly what Solace is designed to counteract.
