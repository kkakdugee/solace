01 — Research

NASA Problem Areas

🧠

Behavioral Health Monitoring

Astronauts experience stress, mood shifts, and emotional fatigue in isolation. Current tools rely on clinical questionnaires that feel like medical paperwork, not personal wellness support.

🌿

Mindfulness & Restoration

No nature walks in space. No ocean sounds, no forests. Astronauts lose access to the restorative environments humans have relied on for thousands of years to manage stress.

🌙

Sleep & Circadian Disruption

The ISS orbits Earth every 90 minutes — 16 sunrises per day. Circadian rhythms are completely disrupted, leading to chronic sleep issues that compound every other risk.
